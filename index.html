devHelp: SA

devHelp is a mobile application that is built to help developers find the job position that is suitable for them. A lot of companies are seeking local developers to satisfy the high demand rate in South Africa. Using this application, employers and employees can benefit equally in a way that suits both sides.

Employees

Can browse through the different opportunities that are hosted by the Companies

Employers

Gets recognition to the company and interest in job applications on a daily basis

Projects

Specific positions are filled more quickly, leading to faster completion for loads of projects

Users

Users get access to a quick and easy to use application that simplifies the dev job search
